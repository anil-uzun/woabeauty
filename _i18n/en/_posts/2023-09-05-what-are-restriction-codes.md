    <h3>What is a Restriction Code?</h3>
    <p>The restriction code is an informative code added to the records of foreigners in our country. Restriction codes can sometimes be added for informational purposes only, sometimes to impose a ban on entry into our country, and in some cases to deport the foreigner.</p>
