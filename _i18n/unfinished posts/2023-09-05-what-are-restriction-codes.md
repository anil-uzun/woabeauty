<h3>What is a Restriction Code?</h3>
<p>Restriction codes are informative codes added to the records of foreigners in Turkey. Restriction codes can sometimes be placed only for informational purposes, sometimes to ban entry to our country, and in some cases to deport foreigners.</p>
